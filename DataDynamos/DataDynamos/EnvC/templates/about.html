About Project

Air and water quality are crucial environmental factors impacting public health and ecological sustainability. Monitoring and visualizing data related to Air Quality Index (AQI) and Water Quality Index (WQI) are essential for informed decision-making and effective environmental management. In this project, we present the development of a comprehensive dashboard using Microsoft Power BI, designed to provide real-time insights into AQI and WQI data.
